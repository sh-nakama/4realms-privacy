Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2025

We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All information you enter into the app stays on your device.

Introduction

4 Realms Wellness Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection

What We DON'T Collect

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not collect usage analytics
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not require account creation or login

What Stays On Your Device

All data you create within the app, including:

  • Activities and activity logs
  • Routines and routine completions
  • Statistics and history

This data is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. We never transmit this data to any server.

Data Storage

Your data is stored exclusively on your device. This means:

  • Your data is as secure as your device
  • If you uninstall the app, your data will be deleted
  • If you clear app data, your information will be removed
  • We cannot access, recover, or restore your data

Data Export

The app provides an export feature that allows you to save your data as a JSON file. This exported file is created and stored locally on your device, entirely under your control, and can be used to backup or transfer your data.

Third-Party Services

4 Realms Wellness Tracker does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Since all data remains on-device and we have no data collection mechanisms, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security features
  • We recommend using device-level security (PIN, fingerprint, face recognition)
  • We recommend regular backups using the export feature

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Export: Download your data anytime using the export feature
  • Delete: Remove any or all data through the app, or by uninstalling
